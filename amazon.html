Welcome to Chembur Karnataka Night College


Chembur Karnataka Night College of Arts, Science and Commerce was established in the year 2018 by Chembur Karnataka Sangha. It is located in Vidyasagar, hardly 10 - 15 minutes walking distance from Chembur railway station. The main aim of Sangha to start Night College is with the sole intention to provide the youth with all possible opportunities of Graduation and enhance educational facility for those who work during day for their livelihood and wish to continue higher education.



Our Motto: ‘Empowerment and illuminating the darkness, through the power of education at Night’



Our Vision: To be recognized as a premier educational Institution for providing Educational opportunities, especially to the lower socio-economic strata of the society and working students who could not get education due to their peculiar problem and instilling values and providing a vibrant environment for the holistic development of students into valuable global citizens.




Our Mission: • Upliftment of working and disadvantaged students by imparting education at night and providing opportunities of career and personality development. • To facilitate higher education within the reach of average, deprived and working students. • To bring holistic development of students by motivating them to participate in various co-curricular and extracurricular activities.



Objectives: • To unlock the higher education opportunities, especially to those who are deprived from it. • To provide a platform for actualizing students’ talent and encourage them to mould their passion into reality.
